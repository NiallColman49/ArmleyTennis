FAQ

The club is located behind houses off Stanningley Road. As the road is a dual carriageway it can be accessed from the east-bound side of the road, between Soap Ridge Road and Cockshott Lane. There is a turning gap in the dual carriageway if heading west. The club is also on the 72 and x6 bus routes and the Leeds to Bradford cycle highway.

Members are permitted to invite guests to play, subject to payment of a visitor's fee of £3.50 per visit. A particular guest may play at the club for a maximum of 3 times, after which they will be expected to become a member. Guest names and their guest fees can be added and paid via a booking when the court is booked by the website .

If you have a compliment or complaint, please contact a member of the committee or email info@armleytennis.net with full details, and your information will be discussed at the next committee meeting or earlier if the matter is urgent. Contact details of committee members can be found on the contacts section.
